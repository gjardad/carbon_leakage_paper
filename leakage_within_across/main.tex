\section{Leakage}\label{sec:leakage}

We test two margins of leakage. Section~\ref{sec:domestic} estimates buyer-side reallocation across domestic ETS-regulated and unregulated suppliers on the Belgian B2B firm-to-firm panel; Section~\ref{sec:international} estimates buyer-side reallocation across EU and non-EU sources of regulated goods on the Belgian customs panel. Both sections share the same identifying event (the 2015 MSR reform, with a Phase~II 2008 alternative for the domestic test), the same definition of regulated products and suppliers, and the same approach to pre-trend and clustering concerns. Their substantive question is the same and their data are complementary: B2B records the within-Belgium share of the leakage channel; customs records the cross-border share.
