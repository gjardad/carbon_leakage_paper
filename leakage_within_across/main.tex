\section{Leakage}\label{sec:leakage}

We test three margins of reallocation. Within NACE 4-digit (\S\ref{sec:domestic_within_intensive}), buyers can shift purchases from more-exposed to less-exposed regulated suppliers in the same product market. Across NACE 4-digit (\S\ref{sec:domestic_across}), buyers can shift expenditure from regulated input categories to unregulated ones. Across international sources (\S\ref{sec:international}), buyers can shift regulated-product imports from EU sources to non-EU sources. The three margins exhaust the within-economy reallocation channel and the canonical cross-border channel. The result is null on all three.
